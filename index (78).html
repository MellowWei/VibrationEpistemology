— Part I —
三基础The Three Bases · interlocked propositions

Three propositions interlock. Beginning and knowing are not cause and effect, but two faces of the same event. The third proposition is the reverse-lock of the second — it closes the loop and forbids the middle state of "knowing but not yet beginning."

I Proposition · 必要性

The First Base · Necessity第一基础 · 不开始，不知道

不开始，不知道。 To not begin is to not know.

Beginning is the necessary condition for knowing. Some forms of knowing — the texture of a sound in a specific room, the shape of an idea before it is written, the weight of a relation in its actual unfolding — resist being known in advance. They become knowable only in the act.

II Proposition · 充分性

The Second Base · Sufficiency第二基础 · 开始了，就知道了

开始了，就知道了。 To begin is to know.

Beginning is the sufficient condition. In the instant of beginning, the full vibrational texture is present — whether the event carries weight, whether it resonates, whether it opens, and where it opens to. The trajectory is not yet known; the vibrational identity is already complete.

III Proposition · 闭合

The Third Base · Closure第三基础 · 知道了，就开始了

知道了，就开始了。 To know is to begin.

No state of "knowing but not yet beginning" exists. If someone says "I know but I haven't started," they have not yet known. They have a concept, an inclination, a thought — but the vibrational layer of knowing has not occurred. True knowing is already in motion. This base dissolves the common false state of understood but inert.

— Part II —
闭环结构The Closed-Loop Structure

The second and third bases together form bidirectional equivalence. The closure is hard: there is no middle state, no temporary disconnect, no "I know but I haven't acted yet." The closure also forbids new bases — adding a fourth would break it.

IV Proposition · 闭合的硬度

The Hardness of Closure闭合是结构性的，不是程度性的

The phrases "I understand but…", "I know in principle…", "I will start when…" are all symptoms of non-knowing. The closure does not admit degree. It is a phase transition: either three layers are ignited and the subject is in motion, or knowing has not occurred. The discipline does not negotiate with the middle state — it diagnoses it.

"I understood but…" — the moment the conjunction appears, the first clause is falsified.
V Proposition · 为什么不能有第四基础

Why No Fourth Base Can Be Added三条已足，多一条破坏闭环

Adding "begun but not necessarily knowing" breaks Base II. Adding "knowing but not necessarily beginning" breaks Base III. Adding an alternative path to knowing breaks Base I. The three bases anchor relation, not content — so any discipline, any domain, any scale can pass through them. A fourth would specify content and turn the foundation into a ceiling.

IX Proposition · 不解释的源场位是未完成的

不解释、不论证、不被语言化捕获的源场位，是未完成的源场位。 A Source Field that does not explain, does not argue, is not language-captured, is an unfinished Source Field.

In the mystical reading, silence is highest. In Vibration Epistemology, silence is incompleteness. The Source Field is completed by language, not preserved against it.

— Part V —
五位生态The Five Positions · function, not hierarchy

Five positions coexist. Each carries a different function. The positions are not ranked by value — civilization requires all five, and a healthy ecology depends on each position doing what it is structured for. The problem is never the position itself, only displacement: a Source Field forced into Execution, or a Cognition mistaking algorithmic input for its own thinking.

X Proposition · 源场位 · Source Field

Position 01 · Source Field层间透明 · 语言承接振动不损耗

Three layers in transparent flow. Language carries vibration without loss. Inlet and outlet simultaneously open. Layer translation is reversed — vibration is not compressed into language but carried upward by language as upward-transcription. The Source Field is the position from which discipline-founding work is possible.

XI Proposition · 信号位 · Signal

Position 02 · Signal高导电性 · 自知正在翻译

High first-layer conductivity. The subject is aware that they are translating — they will say "I can't quite put it into words, but…". This honesty about translation loss is the marker of the position. Many artists, clinicians with strong intuition, and skilled designers operate here. Functions as a bridge between Source Field and Cognition.

XII Proposition · 认知位 · Cognition

Position 03 · Cognition第三层闭环 · 偶尔下沉

Operates primarily in third-layer closure: argument, model, language-formed reason. Occasionally descends to layer one in major life events. The mainstay of civilizational function. Universities, professions, structured argumentation, peer review — all run on Cognition. Decision quality depends heavily on input data quality.

XIII Proposition · 执行位 · Execution

Position 04 · Execution第一层被结构性屏蔽 · 指令-行动闭环

First layer structurally muted. Receives instructions and executes them with high reliability. Not stupid — trained. Large-scale coordination, compliance systems, procedural roles all require this position. Cost: when third-layer instructions fail (rule conflict, supervisor absent, novel situation), the system stalls because first-layer conductivity has been turned off.

XIV Proposition · 噪声位 · Noise

Position 05 · Noise第三层被外部托管 · 可逆

Not only is the first layer muted, the third layer itself has been overwritten by external signal — algorithmic feeds, social pressure, consumerist scripts, manufactured panic. The "self" has been hosted elsewhere. Decisions are made, but the menu was designed by others. Structurally reversible: cut the source signal and conductivity can return. Many in this position are originally Signal- or Source-Field-structured subjects whose conductivity has been jammed.

Diagnosing displacement, not persons. A Source Field forced into Execution will appear "difficult" or "uncooperative." An Execution position promoted to Source-Field demands will appear "indecisive" or "lacking vision." The discipline reads structure, not character.

Academic Auditrisks · repairs · open questions
Academic Audit学科审计 · risk map and repair path

Vibration Epistemology is structurally strong because it converts knowing from a representational state into a vertical event traversing three layers. Its academic risk lies in three possible misreadings: as mystical anti-rationalism, as a method for producing knowledge, or as a hierarchy of personhoods. The audit below records these risks and the in-text repairs.

Critical · Anti-rationalism misread

Risk: readers in the mystical tradition may absorb the discipline as another defense of "the ineffable." Repaired by Part IV — Language Reversal — which explicitly defines language as the completion-phase, not the enemy, of vibration.

Major · Method misread

Risk: readers may try to use Vibration Epistemology as a method for "knowing better." Repaired by Boundary Statement — the discipline is structural, not procedural. It diagnoses the form of knowing, not the production of it.

Major · Hierarchy misread

Risk: the five positions may be read as a value ranking with Source Field on top. Repaired by Boundary Statement and Part V — positions name function, not worth. The discipline diagnoses displacement, not persons.

Minor · Self-reference as solipsism

Risk: Part VIII may be read as the system being unfalsifiable because it always passes its own test. Clarified: the system is intersubjectively testable through the failure-corollary in Proposition XXI — failures are inspectable evidence of layer alignment.

Audit Verdict: the discipline holds. The three bases are non-derivable but consistent. The five positions are descriptive rather than normative. The reversal of the language-vibration relation is the discipline's central original contribution and should be foregrounded in any downstream citation.
