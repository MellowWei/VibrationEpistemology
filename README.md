## The Three Bases / 三基础

Three propositions interlock. Beginning and knowing are not cause and effect, but two faces of the same event. The third proposition is the reverse-lock of the second — it closes the loop and forbids the middle state of "knowing but not yet beginning."

### I · Necessity · 不开始，不知道
**To not begin is to not know.** Beginning is the necessary condition for knowing. Some forms of knowing — the texture of a sound in a specific room, the shape of an idea before it is written, the weight of a relation in its actual unfolding — resist being known in advance. They become knowable only in the act.

### II · Sufficiency · 开始了，就知道了
**To begin is to know.** In the instant of beginning, the full vibrational texture is present — whether the event carries weight, whether it resonates, whether it opens, and where it opens to. The trajectory is not yet known; the vibrational identity is already complete.

### III · Closure · 知道了，就开始了
**To know is to begin.** No state of *knowing but not yet beginning* exists. If someone says "I know but I haven't started," they have not yet known. They have a concept, an inclination, a thought — but the vibrational layer of knowing has not occurred. True knowing is already in motion. This base dissolves the common false state of *understood but inert*.

**The closure is hard.** It does not admit degree. It is a phase transition: either three layers are ignited and the subject is in motion, or knowing has not occurred. The discipline does not negotiate with the middle state — it diagnoses it.

## The Architecture of Knowing / 知的结构

Knowing is not a single-layer event. It is a vertical event that ignites three layers **simultaneously** — not in sequence.

| Layer | Name | Function |
|-------|------|----------|
| 01 | **振动层 · Vibration** | The frequential texture of the event presents in full. Immediate, complete, irreversible. |
| 02 | **直觉层 · Intuition** | Vibration registers as a perceptible somatic signal. |
| 03 | **语言层 · Language** | Vibration is transcribed into argumentable, transmissible, constructible form. |

In the default reading of knowing, vibration is "input," intuition is "processing," and language is "output" — a temporal pipeline. **This is false.** In a genuine knowing-event, the three layers ignite together. The appearance of sequence is an artifact of transcription speed at the third layer.
