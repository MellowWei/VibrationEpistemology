Abstractproblem / approach / contribution
Abstract≤200 words · 中英双语正式摘要

Vibration Epistemology addresses a recurring problem in epistemology: knowing is often reduced either to propositional possession, psychological processing, tacit skill, or mystical immediacy. This discipline proposes a different structure. Its approach begins from the axiom Beginning ⇎ Knowing, where the symbol marks a source-field bidirectional closure rather than a logical biconditional. Knowing is defined as a three-layer simultaneous event: the vibrational layer presents the event’s source-field texture, the intuitive layer registers it as perceivable signal, and the linguistic layer completes it as transmissible form. The contribution is a terminology-safe epistemic foundation for the Vibration as Existence V7.4 monograph: it distinguishes vibration from acoustic, emotional, or New Age readings; separates Source Field ontology from Source Field position; and clarifies five-position ecology as functional structure rather than hierarchy.

《振动认识论》处理认识论中的一个核心误读：知道常被降维为命题拥有、心理加工、默会技能或神秘直觉。本学科提出另一种结构。其方法从公理 开始 ⇎ 知道 出发，其中 ⇎ 表示源场层的双向闭合关系，而不是逻辑双条件。知道被定义为三层同步事件：振动层呈现事件的源场频率结构，直觉层把它登记为可感信号，语言层将其完成为可传递形式。本学科的贡献是为《振动即存在》V7.4 正式版本提供术语安全的认识论根：区分振动与声学/情绪/New Age 读法，区分源场本体与源场位，并将五位生态锁定为功能结构而非价值等级。
